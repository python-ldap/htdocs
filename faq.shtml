  5. Q: ./configure complains about not finding the OpenLDAP libs although they are in place. The following message is found in config.log: 

    /usr/local/openldap1/lib/libldap.so: undefined reference to
`__dn_expand'
/usr/local/openldap1/lib/libldap.so: undefined reference to
`__res_search'
/usr/local/openldap1/lib/libldap.so: undefined reference to
`_getshort'

    A: You have to explicitly link against libresolv: 

    export "LDFLAGS=-lresolv"
./configure
